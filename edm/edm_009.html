Section 03 — Industry Insight
Hybrid Events & Live Streaming for HK Gov
Engage More: Hybrid Events Boost Gov Reach
Discover how event tech and live streaming are transforming public engagement.

Hybrid events are revolutionizing public engagement, demonstrating 50% better attendance rates and 71% higher registration than pure in-person events [1]. This trend highlights the power of technology to extend reach and enhance participation. By combining physical and virtual elements, government departments can overcome geographical barriers and engage a broader audience, fostering greater transparency and inclusivity. Live streaming, now the third most popular video content type, further amplifies this, reaching 27.7% of users weekly [2].

  • Expand reach:** Connect with citizens who cannot attend in-person due to location or time constraints.
  • Increase engagement:** Utilize interactive features like live Q&A and polls to foster dynamic participation.
  • Enhance accessibility:** Provide options for diverse audiences, including those with mobility challenges or specific needs.
  • Data-driven insights:** Gather valuable data on audience preferences and engagement for future planning.

Source: [1] Rozie Synopsis (2025), [2] Switchboard Live (2025)
