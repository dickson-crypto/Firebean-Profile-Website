SECTION 03 — INDUSTRY INSIGHT

Roving Exhibitions: Boosting Gov Outreach

Mobile Outreach: The Future of Government Engagement

Discover how dynamic roving exhibitions are transforming public outreach for HK government departments.

A recent study by OOH TODAY (June 22, 2026) on mobile billboard advertising campaigns revealed a significant increase in audience reach and engagement compared to static displays. This trend extends to government public outreach, where roving exhibitions are proving to be a highly effective strategy. By bringing information and services directly to diverse communities, these mobile formats overcome geographical barriers and enhance accessibility, fostering greater public participation and understanding of government initiatives. Their adaptability allows for targeted engagement in various districts, maximizing impact and resource allocation.

→ Increased Accessibility:** Reach underserved communities and those less likely to visit fixed venues.
→ Enhanced Engagement:** Facilitate direct, interactive experiences tailored to local demographics.
→ Cost-Effectiveness:** Optimize resource deployment by targeting specific areas with higher relevance.
→ Data-Driven Optimization:** Gather real-time feedback for continuous improvement of outreach strategies.

Source: OOH TODAY, June 22, 2026.
