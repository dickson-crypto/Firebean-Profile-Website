Section 03 — Industry Insight
HK Smart Retail & Gov't Promotions
HK's Smart Retail Boom: Gov't Promotion Opportunities
Unlock the power of Hong Kong's digital retail evolution for impactful government campaigns.

Hong Kong's digital payment landscape is rapidly expanding, with the total transaction value projected to reach USD 111.70 billion by 2025 [1]. This significant growth, fueled by government initiatives like the Faster Payment System (FPS) and e-voucher schemes, is transforming the retail sector. Consumers are increasingly embracing contactless payments, mobile POS solutions, and e-commerce, moving away from traditional cash usage. This trend presents unique opportunities for government-linked promotions to engage citizens and support local businesses.

  • Leverage established digital payment platforms (e.g., Octopus, FPS) for seamless public service transactions and campaign payouts.
  • Utilize smart retail data analytics to design highly targeted and effective promotional campaigns that resonate with modern consumers.
  • Partner with retailers to encourage AI adoption and digital transformation, fostering a more dynamic and resilient local economy.
  • Develop integrated online-to-offline (O2O) experiences for government-linked events, enhancing citizen engagement and convenience.

Source: [1] Theseus Solutions, "The Rise of Digital Payments in Hong Kong’s Trade Sector", 2025.
