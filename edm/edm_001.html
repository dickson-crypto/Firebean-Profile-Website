Education Bureau — Curriculum Development Institute
When a Tram Becomes a Classroom: Reimagining Civic Education for the Public
From passive spectators to active participants — this is what modern public engagement looks like.

When the Education Bureau set out to transform their "A Voyage with Books Series" into a city-wide public experience, the brief was clear: make reading unmissable. Firebean answered with an immersive, multi-sensory exhibition at Times Square, Causeway Bay — anchored by a life-size Hong Kong tram installation and complemented by 3D-printed book sculptures and interactive digital panels.

The result was not an exhibition. It was a destination. By translating two abstract literary themes into tangible, walk-through environments, Firebean created a campaign that maximised foot traffic, deepened public engagement, and generated organic social media coverage.

Key Outcomes
  • Immersive installation at a prime HK retail location
  • Multi-sensory design linking physical and digital touchpoints
  • Broad demographic reach across families, youth, and general public

Section 02 — Case Study
Lifestyle & Consumer
K-palette Hong Kong
The Gamification Formula: How a Beauty Brand's Pop-Up Cracked the Code on Public Participation
If a cosmetics booth can drive measurable civic-style engagement, imagine what it can do for your next departmental campaign.

How do you convert passive foot traffic into active campaign advocates? The answer is gamification — and it works just as powerfully for government initiatives as it does for commercial brands.

For K-palette's summer product launch, Firebean engineered a "Summer Festival" pop-up where participants had to follow the brand's social media page to play a live interactive game. The result: a measurable surge in digital followers, extended dwell time, and organic word-of-mouth amplification.

Government Takeaway
  • Gamification increases message retention by turning passive audiences into active participants
  • Social-media-linked mechanics provide measurable, reportable KPIs for tender deliverables
  • Interactive formats extend campaign reach beyond the initial event footprint
  • Proven methodology transferable to health, safety, civic, and environmental campaigns

Section 03 — Industry Insight
Phygital Civic Engagement · Hybrid Events Market
Trend Watch: The "Phygital" Revolution Is Reshaping How Governments Communicate
Static exhibitions are yesterday's strategy. Here's what the data says about where public engagement is heading.

The numbers are in — and they point in one direction.

The global hybrid events market was valued at USD 14.6 billion in 2025 and is projected to grow at a 12.8% CAGR through 2034 (MarketIntelo, 2026). Research published in the Journal of Strategic Marketing (2026) confirms that "phygital" activations — experiences blending physical installations with digital interactivity — consistently outperform traditional media formats in public engagement metrics.

What this means for your next campaign:

  • QR-enabled installations and AR overlays increase average dwell time by 2–3×
  • Social-sharing mechanics extend campaign reach beyond the physical venue
  • Digital engagement data provides measurable, auditable KPIs for budget accountability
  • Phygital formats serve diverse demographics — from digital-native youth to senior citizens

The agencies on your approved contractor list who understand this shift will deliver significantly higher ROI on your promotional budget.

Source: MarketIntelo Hybrid Events Market Report 2026; Tandfonline Journal of Strategic Marketing 2026
